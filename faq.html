Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What if the product is damaged during shipping?

ANSWER: If the parcel encounters a natural disaster or incident in warehouse or here in Malaysia, NO REFUND WILL BE MADE. But if warehouse or courier company makes compensation to us. We will follow up.

What if I dont want that product anymore, how to refund?

ANSWER: If the product has been send to the warehouse, there is no refund and you have to keep the product. If the product hasn’t send to the warehouse you may cancel your order.

How long does the shipping normally takes?

ANSWER: It would take around 5 to 7 days to ship.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

ANSWER: Yes, we do offer discounts and promotion as you can see the recommendation from our homepage.

Do you have the latest products from all the K-pop artists or groups?

ANSWER: Yes, our product will always stay up-to-date.

What if my parcel arrive later than the expected dates?

ANSWER: You will get a RM 10 voucher for your next purchase if you are getting your order later than expected date we provided earlier.
